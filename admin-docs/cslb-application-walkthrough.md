# CSLB Contractor's License Application -- Treadwell Restoration LLC

This is the critical-path item. Every day of delay pushes first job back by a day.

## Before you start

You need:
- Treadwell Restoration LLC formation confirmation from CA Secretary of State
- EIN letter from IRS
- Jesse's full name, address, DOB, SSN, and current B license number
- Decision made on Jesse's role: Manager (no equity, BQI bond required) vs Member (10%+ equity, BQI exempt)
- A check or credit card for ~$1,500 in fees and bond premiums

## The application

File at: https://www.cslb.ca.gov/OnlineServices/

### Section 1: Business entity

- Entity type: Limited Liability Company
- Entity name: Treadwell Restoration LLC
- DBA: (leave blank unless using one)
- State of formation: California
- LLC ID: (from Secretary of State)
- EIN: (from IRS)
- Business address: (Jesse's Fontana address or your registered agent address)
- Mailing address: (same or PO box)

### Section 2: Classification

- Primary classification: B - General Building Contractor
- (Don't select additional classifications now -- add C-36 plumbing later if Jesse pursues it)

### Section 3: Personnel

- Member 1: Nate Goldstein, 100% ownership (or whatever ownership split you decide)
- Manager 1: Jesse [Last Name], 0% ownership, designated as Responsible Managing Manager (RMM)
- Qualifying Individual: Jesse [Last Name]

### Section 4: Qualifying Individual experience

Jesse already holds an active B license -- he qualifies via "current license held" rather than re-documenting 4 years of experience. The CSLB system should auto-populate his qualifications when you enter his license number.

If for any reason it asks for experience documentation, here's the framing:
- 25+ years as licensed B General Contractor
- Direct supervision of building construction including framing, plumbing, electrical, finish work
- Multiple completed projects per year, residential and commercial

### Section 5: Bonds

Two bonds required because Jesse owns 0% equity:
1. **Contractor License Bond -- $25,000.** Annual premium $200-400 depending on credit. Required for ALL contractor licenses.
2. **Bond of Qualifying Individual -- $25,000.** Annual premium $300-500. Required when qualifier owns less than 10% of the licensed entity.

If Jesse becomes a 10%+ Member, the BQI bond is waived. Decide before filing.

Recommended bond providers: CCIS Bonds, Surety Bonds Direct, or your insurance broker. Get quotes from 2-3.

### Section 6: Workers' compensation

Either:
- Bind a WC policy through State Fund (statefundca.com) before filing -- provide policy number on application
- File a Certificate of Workers' Compensation Self-Insurance Exemption (only valid if you genuinely have zero employees, which won't be true once Jesse is W-2)

Recommend binding the WC policy first.

### Section 7: Fees

- Application fee: $450
- Initial license fee: $200
- Total CSLB fees: $650
- Plus bond premiums (~$500-800 first year)
- Plus WC initial deposit ($300-500)

Total out the door: ~$1,500-2,000

## After submission

1. CSLB sends acknowledgment with application number -- typical 1-2 weeks
2. Jesse takes the Asbestos Open-Book Exam if he hasn't already (likely already done for his existing B license)
3. CSLB processes -- typical 6-9 weeks total
4. License approval -- CSLB sends license number and pocket card
5. Update website footer, business cards, GBP, Yelp with license number (CA law requires this on all advertising)

## What can go wrong

- **Jesse's B license has issues.** Verify status BEFORE filing.
- **Bond underwriting denial.** If credit is poor, premiums may be 2-3x. Shop multiple sureties.
- **Address mismatch.** LLC address, business address, and qualifier address must be consistent.
- **Workers' comp gap.** If you claim exemption now and later add employees, license can be suspended.

## Red flags to escalate

- Application held up beyond 9 weeks -- call CSLB, check application status online
- Bond denial -- talk to a contractor licensing attorney
- Any hint of Jesse's existing license being non-active or in dispute -- STOP, investigate first
