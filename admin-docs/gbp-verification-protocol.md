# Google Business Profile -- Verification Video Protocol

Service Area Businesses (SABs) like ours can't be verified by mail (no public address shown). Video verification is the path. Get this right the first time -- re-submissions delay you 1-2 weeks each.

## When to record

After:
- Truck decals are installed
- You have business cards in hand
- LLC paperwork or business license printed copies available
- You're physically at the business address (Jesse's Fontana location)

## Equipment

- Smartphone with good camera
- **Open Google Maps app on the phone** (not browser, not Photos app -- must be Google Maps)
- Make sure GPS / location services are ON
- Make sure phone storage isn't full (need ~200MB headroom)

## What to record (in this order)

Total length: 60-120 seconds. ONE continuous take. No edits, no cuts, no faces, no sensitive info.

### 1. Outside establishing shot (10-15 sec)
- Stand outside the building
- Pan to show the street, address number on the house/building, recognizable landmarks
- Goal: prove you're at the address registered

### 2. Approach to truck (10-15 sec)
- Walk up to the truck
- Show truck magnets/decals with "Treadwell Restoration" visible
- Linger on the branding -- make sure it's readable on camera

### 3. Open the truck (15-20 sec)
- Open the back/cab
- Show tools and equipment -- moisture meters, air movers, plumbing tools, anything that signals real operations
- Goal: prove this is a working business, not a paper shell

### 4. Business documentation (15-20 sec)
- Pull out and show on camera:
  - LLC formation paperwork (Articles of Organization)
  - Business license (if received)
  - Business card with Treadwell Restoration name + phone
  - Any printed invoice, COI, or letterhead
- Goal: prove the business name on the truck matches the registered business

### 5. Workspace access (10-15 sec)
- Walk into garage, office, or wherever you store equipment
- Show that you have access to the work area
- Goal: prove this is where you actually operate from

### 6. Final establishing shot (5-10 sec)
- Step back, show the truck + workspace + business signage together if possible
- End with steady frame

## What NOT to do

- Don't film faces (Google rejects videos with people's faces)
- Don't film customer information, license numbers in close-up, or anything personally identifiable
- Don't edit the video -- has to be one continuous take
- Don't use a stabilizer or weird camera angles
- Don't go over 2 minutes

## Submission

In Google Maps app or Google Business Profile dashboard:
1. Initiate the verification process
2. Select "Video" as the verification method
3. Upload the recording
4. Submit

## Approval timeline

- Typical: 1-5 business days
- If rejected: you'll get an email with a generic reason. Re-record addressing whatever was unclear and re-submit.

## If rejected

Most common rejection reasons:
- Address not visible enough -- re-record with more emphasis on street signs and building number
- Branding not visible -- re-record with closer shots of truck decals
- No business documentation shown -- re-record with paperwork visible
- Too short -- make sure you hit the 60-second minimum

## After approval

- GBP becomes "live" in Google Maps and Search
- You can now apply for Google Local Services Ads (LSA)
- You can start collecting reviews (don't fake them -- Google will catch it and suspend you)
- You can post updates, photos, hours, etc.
