# Equipment -- Buy List & Rental Plan

## What to buy day 1 (~$2,250)

### Moisture meters -- $400 total
- **Pin moisture meter** -- Protimeter Surveymaster or equivalent, ~$200
- **Pinless moisture meter** -- Tramex or Delmhorst BD-10, ~$200
- Why both: pin for direct readings in materials, pinless for non-destructive scanning of walls

### Thermal camera -- $400
- **FLIR ONE Pro** ($400, plugs into phone) OR **FLIR C5** ($600, standalone)
- For a starter, FLIR ONE Pro is fine
- Used for: finding hidden moisture, checking dry-out progress, customer-facing documentation

### Air movers -- $900 (4-6 units)
- **B-Air Vento** or **Phoenix Axial** -- $150-200 each
- Already have 2-3, so buy 3-4 more for total fleet of 6-7
- Stack-able centrifugal preferred for tight spaces

### Extraction -- $300
- **Weighted extraction tool** (rover-style) -- pulls water from carpet faster than wet vac
- Skip the truck-mount system -- overkill for new business

### PPE & consumables -- $250
- Tyvek suits (case of 25)
- N95 / N100 masks (Cat 2/3 work)
- Nitrile gloves (case)
- Plastic sheeting (6mil, 10ft x 100ft rolls -- get 2)
- Painter's tape (case)
- Antimicrobial (Benefect or Sporicidin starter)
- Containment poles (Zipwall or equivalent -- 2-set)
- Trash bags, contractor-grade

## What to RENT, not buy

### Dehumidifiers -- $0 capital
- LGR (low-grain refrigerant) dehumidifiers run $1,800-2,500 each new
- Rent at $75-125/day from local IE supplier
- Bill rental cost through to insurance/customer
- Buy 1-2 only after you've completed 5+ jobs and know your typical demand

### Air scrubbers / HEPA filtration
- For mold or contaminated water work
- Rent until volume justifies purchase

### Negative air machines
- For containment
- Rent

## What NOT to buy on day 1

- Truck-mounted extraction system ($30K+) -- wait for volume
- Multiple LGR dehumidifiers -- rent until you know capacity needs
- Premium thermal camera ($2K+) -- FLIR ONE Pro is sufficient for the first year
- Air scrubbers -- rent
- Specialty mold equipment -- wait until first mold-specific job
- Chemical inventory beyond starters -- buy as needed per job
